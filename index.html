About Our Zoo

Since 1987, SV Zoo Park has been a sanctuary for wildlife, sprawling across 5,532 acres. Our mission is to inspire conservation through education and unforgettable experiences.

Highlights include:

  • Mayuravani - Vibrant peafowl sanctuary
  • Mrugavani - Haven for herbivores
  • Vrukavihar - Home to smaller carnivores and exotic birds

Our zoo was once home to Balaji, a legendary leopard weighing up to 143 kg, a testament to our commitment to extraordinary wildlife.

Meet Our Animals

Lions

  • Large carnivorous mammals
  • Known for distinctive manes
  • Live in prides
  • Native to Africa and India

Elephants

  • Largest land animals
  • Intelligent social structures
  • Use trunks for multiple tasks
  • Endangered species

Giraffes

  • World's tallest mammals
  • Long necks for high foliage
  • Live in towers
  • Vulnerable species

Monkeys

  • Intelligent primates
  • Diverse habitats globally
  • Agile and social
  • Varied diets

Penguins

  • Flightless aquatic birds
  • Southern Hemisphere natives
  • Form breeding colonies
  • Threatened by climate change

Zebras

  • Distinctive black and white stripes
  • Native to African savannas
  • Live in herds for protection
  • Herbivores feeding on grasses

Tigers

  • Largest big cat species
  • Distinctive orange coat with stripes
  • Solitary hunters
  • Endangered due to poaching

Rhinoceros

  • Massive herbivores with thick skin
  • Known for their horns
  • Native to Africa and Asia
  • Critically endangered

Polar Bears

  • Arctic marine mammals
  • Excellent swimmers and hunters
  • White fur for camouflage
  • Threatened by melting sea ice
